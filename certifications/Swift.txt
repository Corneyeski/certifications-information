DESCRIPTION
============
Bootcamp designed to learn how to develop iOS applications, learning how to create an application, the basics of Swift 4, manage data and design the views

REPOSITORIES
============
1- https://github.com/Corneyeski/MusicBootCampiOS first approach to iOS development
2- https://github.com/Corneyeski/MandoBootcamp final application, a simulated AC with a remote controller and some functions depending on the satus

TIME
============
40 hours in a week.
